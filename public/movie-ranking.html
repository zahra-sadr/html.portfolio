The Best Movies According to Zahra

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A moving tale of friendship, hope, and resilience in the most unlikely of places, featuring standout performances and a poignant narrative that leaves a lasting impact.

The Dark Knight (2008)

A dark and gripping crime drama that transcends the superhero genre, featuring standout performances and a complex portrayal of morality and chaos, anchored by Ledger's unforgettable performance as the Joker.

Casablanca (1942)

A classic romance set against the backdrop of World War II, filled with memorable characters, iconic dialogue, and emotional depth that continues to resonate with audiences worldwide.
